Fish Fry

Ingredients

  • 600 gms Rawas fish fillets
  • 2 tbs Turmeric paste
  • 2 tsp Red chilli powder
  • 1 Â½ tbsp. Ginger garlic powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying

Process

  • Take about 600 gms of Rawas fish (flesh without bones) and make about 12 pieces of filets (flat slice).
  • Mix turmeric paste, red chilli powder, ginger garlic paste and salt together.
  • Allow the fish filets to marinate for 15-20 minutes in the paste.
  • Heat oil in frying pan. Fry the fish till it turns golden brown.
  • Take out the fried fish pieces and put over kitchen napkin to soak the excess oil. Serve hot.
