Mutton Korma

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup oil
  • ½ cup onion paste
  • ½ kg mutton
  • 2 tbsp curd, whisked
  • Salt
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp red chilli powder
  • 1 tsp garlic, roughly chopped
  • 2-3 cloves
  • 2 cardamom pods
  • 1-2 cinnamon sticks
  • Coriander, chopped for garnish

Process

  • Heat oil in a deep pan, add onion paste, and cook for 5-10 minutes or until brown.
  • Now add mutton, whisked curd, salt, coriander powder and red chilli powder to this. Mix well.
  • Let it cook for 5-6 minutes.
  • Add in chopped ginger and garlic, cloves, cardamom pods and cinnamon sticks.
  • Combine everything together.
  • Add water and cover it with a lid and slow cook on medium flame for 40- 60 minutes.
  • Once the meat is tender and completely cooked garnish with chopped coriander and serve hot.
