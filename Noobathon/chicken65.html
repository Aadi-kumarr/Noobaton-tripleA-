Chicken 65

Ingredients

  1. For Marination
    • 250 gms chicken, cut in cubes
    • ¼ cup yogurt
    • 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste
    • 1 tbsp curry leaves, chopped
    • Black pepper powder to taste
    • 1 tsp cumin powder
    • Salt to taste
    • 2 tsp red chilli powder
    • 2 tbsp rice flour
    • Oil to deep fry
  2. For Tempering
    • 1 tbsp oil
    • 2 green chillies, slit
    • 1 tbsp garlic, chopped
    • 10-12 curry leaves
    • Black pepper powder to taste

Process

  • Add chicken, yogurt, ginger garlic paste, curry leaves, black pepper powder, cumin powder, salt and red chilli powder.
  • Mix and coat the chicken well. Set aside to marinate for 30 minutes. Add rice flour after 30 minutes and mix well.
  • Heat oil in a kadhai and deep fry the marinated chicken till golden and crisp.
  • Drain on an absorbent paper and set aside.
  • Heat oil in a pan, add green chillies, garlic and curry leaves. Sauté till fragrant
  • Add the fried chicken and toss well. Add black pepper powder and toss well.
  • Serve hot
